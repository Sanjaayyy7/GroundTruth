# MiniJudge — Threats to Validity

Prose mirror of `docs/threats.yaml` (contract CT8 keeps the ID sets
identical). MiniJudge is a 12-item evaluation built as the second consumer
for Groundtruth's Meta-Evaluation Engine; its threats are correspondingly
small, but they are real and they are registered.

| ID | Category | Threat |
|---|---|---|
| T1 | statistical | 12 items cannot support generalization beyond this labeled set |
| T2 | statistical | Reference verdicts and judge share one author |
| T3 | construct | Negation keywords are a proxy for semantic refutation |
